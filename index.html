About Us

Who We Are

We are DBS Building Services, a Cheltenham based Association of Master Tradesmen accredited company founded in 2014. Our ethos has always revolved around utilising local, trusted and skilled tradesmen.

What We Do

We are able to carry out a multitude of services, including but not limited to new builds, extensions, rebuilds, refinishing and creating initial plans. Please see our gallery for examples of our capabilities.

Our Core Values

We pride ourselves on application of our core values on each and every job. These are integrity, experience, commitment to quality, building of relationships and reputation.

DBS Design

Quality every step of the way

At DBS Design Services, we proudly offer quality services to our clients from the start. From your initial idea, we are able to create full plans ready for planning permission, and detailed ray-traced renders using state of the art software. We can help you visualise exactly how your project, regardless of size, will look. Visit our gallery to see examples of our plans and renders.
